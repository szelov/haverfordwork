About High Noon Music and its authors

High Noon Music is a project by Payton, Alex, and Scottie. We consider outselves to be one of the most mediocre music websites out there on the "web". We post and talk about music that we have either found recently or just always have liked to listen to. Over time we hope to host music files for streaming and downloading, and have a members only area which you will need an account to access.

Happy hunting!
High Noon could be considered the most mediocre music project website of this century.

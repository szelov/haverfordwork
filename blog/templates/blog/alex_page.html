Hello, and welcome to my webpage about my favorite music. My goal is expose you to the greatest music of all time form manny different genres.

with bands like Coldplay all the back to the 70's and 80's with bands like ACDC and Guns and Roses. The second genre is Old School Rap, consisting of rap songs that were released begore 2000. You can expect to see artis like 2Pac, Biggie and the Wu-Tang Clan. Third we have Modern Day Rap. You can expect to find artist like eminem, Jay Z, Lil Wayne and more. And Last we have Electronic Dance Music also know as EDM. Here you can expect to find Djs like the Chainsmoker, Dj Snake, Major Lazer and many more.
